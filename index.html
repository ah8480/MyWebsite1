World Cup

What is the world cup?

The World Cup in football is a quadrennial tournament of 32 men's national teams it happens every 4 years, that is organized by the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA). It determines the sport's men's world champion.

How was the world cup created?

The first World Cup took place back in 1930 in uraguay, and was proposed by Jules Rimet, who wanted to create an international competition between men's football teams
